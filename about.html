About Us

Trinity Relief Initiative For Returnees And Migrants

We the founders of Trinity Relief Initiative for Returnees and Migrants are a group of selected individuals who have been victims of human trafficking and have experienced near-death experiences. We were saved by grace and lucky to make it to safety alive. While on the journey of being trafficked, we decided to save as many as possible and to sensitize the public on the risk of irregular migration. We have turned our circumstances around for positive change.

Our Vision

To become a leading organization in Nigeria, empowering returnees and migrants to rebuild their lives with dignity, while fostering a culture of inclusivity and community resilience.

Our Mission

The Trinity Relief Initiative for Returnees and Migrants aims to provide comprehensive support to migrants and returnees, promoting their economic self-sufficiency, social stability, and psychosocial well-being through reintegration programs and sustainable developmental goals.

From providing essential resources to funding life-changing projects, every effort is directed toward building a better, more equitable world. By uniting individuals, businesses, and communities.
