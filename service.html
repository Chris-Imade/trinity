✨ Our Services

Empowering Communities

Comprehensive support services designed to facilitate successful reintegration and sustainable development for returnees and communities.

Reintegration Assistance

Comprehensive support for returnees to achieve economic stability and social integration through tailored programs and resources.

Psychosocial Support

Professional counseling and mental health services to help returnees overcome trauma and rebuild their emotional well-being.

Livelihood Development

Skill acquisition and entrepreneurship training to empower returnees with sustainable income-generating opportunities.

Community Engagement

Building strong support networks and fostering community acceptance for returnees through awareness programs and local partnerships.

Advocacy

Raising awareness about the risks of irregular migration and advocating for the rights and needs of returnees and migrants.

Sensitization Programs

Educational initiatives to prevent irregular migration by informing communities about the dangers and providing safer alternatives.
