About Us

Trinity Relief Initiative For Returnees And Migrants

We the founders of Trinity Relief Initiative for Returnees and Migrants are a group of selected individuals who have been victims of human trafficking and have experienced near-death experiences. We were saved by grace and lucky to make it to safety alive, while on the journey of being trafficked and have decided to save as many as possible and to sensitize the public on the risk of irregular migration. We have turned the circumstance around for a positive change.

Our Vision

To become a leading organization in Nigeria, empowering returnees and migrants to rebuild their lives with dignity, while fostering a culture of inclusivity and community resilience.

Our Mission

The Trinity Relief Initiative for Returnees and Migrants aims to provide comprehensive support to migrants and returnees, promoting their economic self-sufficiency, social stability, and psychosocial well-being through reintegration programs and sustainable developmental goals.

Reintegration Assistance

Providing support for returnees to achieve economic stability and social integration.

Psychosocial Support

Offering mental health services and counseling to promote emotional well-being.

At the heart of our organization lies a powerful mission: to provide comprehensive support and sustainable solutions for returnees and migrants, helping them rebuild their lives with dignity and hope.

Testimonials

Stories of Hope and Recovery

Real stories from returnees and migrants who have rebuilt their lives
with the support of Trinity Relief Initiative.

"After returning from Libya, I felt lost and hopeless. Trinity Relief Initiative helped me learn tailoring skills and provided counseling. Today, I have my own small business and can support my family. Their support changed my life completely."

Aisha Okonkwo

Returnee from Libya

"The sensitization program saved me from making a dangerous journey. I learned about the risks of irregular migration and received training in catering. Now I'm running a successful food business in my community."

Chidinma Eze

Beneficiary

"The psychological support I received helped me overcome the trauma of my experience. The organization didn't just help me survive; they helped me thrive. I'm now a volunteer, helping others find their path to recovery."

Oluwaseun Adebayo

Returnee & Volunteer

"Through the skill acquisition program, I learned hairdressing and now have my own salon. The organization's support gave me a second chance at life. I'm grateful for their commitment to helping people like me rebuild their lives."

Folake Ogunleye

Skill Acquisition Beneficiary

"The community engagement programs helped me understand the dangers of irregular migration. I'm now an advocate in my community, sharing my story to prevent others from making the same mistakes I almost made."
