Our Comprehensive Support

Empowering Lives Through Holistic Assistance

We provide comprehensive support to various vulnerable groups, addressing their unique needs and challenges with innovative solutions and compassionate care.

Returnees & Migrants

Comprehensive reintegration support including skill acquisition, psychosocial counseling, and economic empowerment programs to help rebuild lives with dignity.

Victims of Trafficking

Specialized support for VOTs including trauma counseling, legal assistance, and long-term rehabilitation programs to ensure sustainable recovery.

Gender-Based Violence

Dedicated programs for GBV survivors including safe spaces, medical support, legal aid, and economic empowerment initiatives.

Climate Change Adaptation

Training in sustainable agriculture, renewable energy, and climate-smart practices to build resilience against environmental challenges.

Skill Development

Vocational training in various sectors including renewable energy, sustainable agriculture, and digital skills for modern employment.

Community Engagement

Sensitization programs on irregular migration risks, climate change awareness, and sustainable development practices.
